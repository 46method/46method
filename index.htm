4:6 Method


A Coffee Brewing Theory
(Invented by Tetsu Kasuya)

Adjust the taste by dividing the water into a 4:6 ratio
1. The first 40% adjusts the balance of sweetness and acidity
2. The remaining 60% adjusts the strength

Settings

  • Ground coffee
    15g
  • A pour of water
    45ml
  • Total water
    225ml
  • Pouring time
    45sec
  • Balance
    of sweetness & acidity
